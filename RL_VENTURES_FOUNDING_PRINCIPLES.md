## Core Principles

### 1. Verify Before You Act
*Confidence must be earned, not assumed.*

In code: formal proofs before deployment.  
In clay: test the form before firing.  
In life: know the ground before you move.

### 2. Shape With Intent
*Everything we make has purpose.*

No accidental features. No wasted motion.  
Form follows function follows proof.

### 3. Ground the Abstract
*Ideas mean nothing until they touch reality.*

Theory → simulation → physical.  
Concept → prototype → product.  
The bridge between thought and thing.

### 4. Truth Over Comfort
*We build what works, not what looks good on paper.*

Honest materials. Honest code. Honest results.

### 5. Make What Lasts
*Craft for permanence, not trends.*

Pottery that survives generations.  
Systems that survive adversarial conditions.  
Work that matters in 50 years.
