TT&P delivers end-to-end power backup solutions for telecommunication sites — from initial engineering and equipment procurement through installation, commissioning, and long-term preventive maintenance.

DC Power Plants

Design, installation, and commissioning of -48VDC and +24VDC rectifier systems. We work with all major platforms and integrate with existing site alarms and SCADA infrastructure.

  • Emerson NetSure, GE, Eltek Flatpack2, Alpha FXM
  • Rectifier module replacement & capacity expansion
  • Power distribution frame (PDF) installation
  • Remote power cabinets & RRUS power
  • Alarm system integration & commissioning

Battery Systems

Full lifecycle support for all battery chemistries — procurement, installation, capacity testing, thermal management, and scheduled replacement programs to ensure runtime compliance.

  • VRLA / AGM / Gel Cell installation & replacement
  • Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) systems
  • NiCd battery systems for extreme environments
  • Capacity discharge & impedance testing (MIDTRONICS, MEGGER)
  • Float voltage verification & thermal audits
  • Battery replacement & recycling programs

Generator Systems

Complete generator installation, ATS integration, load testing, and preventive maintenance to ensure reliable backup power when the grid fails.

  • Diesel generator installation (Cat, Cummins, Kohler, MTU)
  • Natural gas & propane alternatives
  • Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) install & testing
  • Load bank testing & commissioning
  • Scheduled PM programs & fluid services
  • Fuel polishing & tank management

Fuel Cell Systems

Hydrogen and methanol PEM fuel cell installation for sites requiring clean, silent, long-duration backup power — ideal where generator use is restricted or impractical.

  • Hydrogen PEM fuel cell systems
  • Methanol / reformed fuel cell platforms
  • Indoor & outdoor enclosure configurations
  • Integration with existing DC power plants
  • Zero-emission backup power compliance

UPS Systems

Online and line-interactive UPS installation for AC-powered telecom and network equipment, ensuring seamless power continuity during grid interruptions.

  • Online double-conversion UPS installation
  • Bypass switching & redundancy configurations
  • Runtime extension with external battery modules
  • UPS battery replacement & PM programs
  • Commissioning & load testing

Remote Monitoring

SNMP-based power monitoring integration for real-time visibility into the health of every power asset across your entire site portfolio.

  • Temperature, voltage & current monitoring
  • Alarm management & escalation routing
  • Remote battery health diagnostics
  • Predictive maintenance alerting
  • Integration with existing NOC platforms

FCC Backup Power Compliance — We Know It Cold.

FCC Order 12-72 and subsequent rules require commercial mobile radio service (CMRS) providers to maintain minimum backup power at cell sites and central offices. Compliance involves battery runtime calculations, documentation, testing protocols, and reporting. TT&P assesses your current compliance posture and brings every site up to standard — with full documentation to support your FCC filings.
